Privacy Policy — Sushi Tracker

Last updated: 18 July 2026

Sushi Tracker ("the app") is a simple tally counter. This policy explains what the app does — and does not do — with your information.

Data we collect

None. Sushi Tracker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

Data stored on your device

Your counts, sessions, and lifetime totals are saved locally on your device only, using on-device storage. This data never leaves your phone. Uninstalling the app, or clearing its data from your device settings, permanently removes it.

Network access

The app does not connect to the internet. It contains no ads, no third-party tracking or advertising SDKs, and no crash-reporting services.

Permissions

Sushi Tracker requests no special device permissions (no location, contacts, camera, microphone, or storage-access permissions).

Children

The app is safe for all ages. Because it collects no data whatsoever, it collects no data from children either.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a new "Last updated" date.
